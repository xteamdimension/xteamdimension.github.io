Scroll Craft

11 Oct 2013

Scrollcraft has been developed in 48hours (including the nights!), during the international Indie Speed Run 2013.

It is an endless runner featuring the theme and the element assigned to the team: Speed and Scroll.

So we drew Nocht, an indie game developer, running away from a "scrolling scroll" and jumping on word platforms appearing during the game.

Scrollcraft placed 4th in two categories: "Best Animation" and "Best 2D Graphics", during the ISR2013-Rome award.

The game will be officially released on December 25, 2013, but, if you want, you can try Scrollcraft (warning: alpha-quality!) released as-was at the end of the 48h competition, after tons of Coke and junk-food.
